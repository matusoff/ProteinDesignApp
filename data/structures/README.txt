Optional structure files for demos (AlphaFold 3, etc.)

Place mmCIF or CIF here with one of these basenames (extension .mmcif or .cif):

  Human_ubiquitin_mature
  Human_alpha-synuclein
  Demo_HCV_NS3_Nterm

When you click "Load demo" and pick the matching sequence, the app will attach
this file automatically if it exists. Otherwise use "Coordinates" upload as usual.

Large AF3 models are not committed to the repo — add your own exports locally.
