# Disclaimer

**Protein Developability Review Assistant** is a **research / educational prototype**. It is **not** a medical device, not a regulated bioinformatics product, and **not** validated for use in regulatory submissions, clinical decisions, or GMP contexts.

- Outputs are **heuristic** and **exploratory**. They do **not** replace experimental developability assays, formulation studies, or expert review.
- **Mutation suggestions** are illustrative first-pass ideas only. They must be evaluated in light of structure, function, immunogenicity, manufacturability, and program-specific constraints.
- **Structure-aware** features (when coordinates are supplied) depend on uploaded models and optional server-side rendering (e.g. PyMOL). They do not certify structural accuracy of those models.
